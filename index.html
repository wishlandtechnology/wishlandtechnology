About Wishland Technology

Our Mission
To enable sustainable farming and resilient livelihoods through easy-to-use apps and media streaming tools designed for field conditions.

We combine lightweight mobile apps, low-bandwidth streaming and on-field training to help farmers make better decisions. Our products are built for low-connectivity environments and focused on practical outcomes: higher yields, lower waste and stronger communities.

  • Local-language content delivery via streaming
  • Crop advisory through simple mobile apps
  • Market-linkage and price alerts
